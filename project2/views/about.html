About Me

4X National Champion
7X International Champion

Hi! My name is Faris Karkelja, a 20-year-old salsa dancer based in Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina. In 2021, I had the opportunity to showcase my talent on the mesmerizing platform of the Supertalent Show held in Zagreb, the capital city of Croatia.

This experience was a crucial moment in my dance career, igniting a renewed sense of motivation that has fueled my continuous growth and improvement ever since.

Apart from my passion for the art of dance, I have a deep interest in various technological domains. Alongside my dance journey, I am currently engaged in higher education, focusing on Information Technology at the university level.
